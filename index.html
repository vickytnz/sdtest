Answer 1, 2, or 3

Not everyone is looking to undergo radical change, and sometimes it isn’t needed. Because service design engages stakeholders in a new way, asking different questions, it might reveal that something needs to look and feel very different from how you imagined. To get the most out of working with a service designer, it is better to be in a position where you would be able to implement radical change if it was needed.
